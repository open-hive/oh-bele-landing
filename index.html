FAQ

Common Questions

What areas does Bele Technologies cover?
We are headquartered in Gaborone and serve clients across all of Botswana. Our five practice areas cover IT Hardware Supply, IT Support & Maintenance, Custom Software Development, Cloud & Managed Services, and Immersive AR/VR technology.
How quickly can I expect a response to my enquiry?
Our average response time for all client enquiries and support requests is under 2 hours. We pride ourselves on accessibility, responsiveness, and reliability, you can reach us by phone, cell, or email.
Do you work with government and public sector clients?
Yes. We have extensive experience working with government ministries, parastatals, councils, and research bodies. We understand public sector procurement processes and compliance requirements, and our pricing is structured to suit public sector budgets.
What does your AR/VR service include?
Our immersive technology practice covers the full XR delivery chain: custom VR/AR content development, 360° video production, XR headset supply (Meta Quest, Pico, and more), pre-configuration with client content, on-site technical support, and synchronized multi-headset VR event delivery.
Can you handle small business and startup projects?
Absolutely. We serve clients of all sizes, from large government ministries to individual entrepreneurs and early-stage startups. Our agile approach means personalised attention and flexible solutions regardless of project size.
Do you offer managed IT service contracts?
Yes. We offer managed service contracts with defined response and resolution times, including helpdesk support, system health monitoring, preventive maintenance, and IT asset lifecycle planning, tailored to your specific operational needs.
